## 1. Strategic Leadership: President

### Main Functions

#### Strategic Direction
- **Establishes** ACIK's strategic priorities and defines the key directions of the Association's activities
- **Sets** the long-term vision for Italian-Kazakh relations
- **Provides** guidance on positioning and brand strategy
- **Ensures** alignment with national interests of both countries

#### Strategic Approval
- **Endorses** the annual program of initiatives and events
- **Sets** relative priorities among competing activities
- **Approves** major strategic decisions (new markets, major partnerships, significant investments)
- **Reviews** annual budget and strategic plan

#### High-Level Representation
- **Represents** the Association at the international level
- **Engages** with heads of state, ministers, and senior officials
- **Participates** in high-level bilateral forums
- **Maintains** relationships with ambassadors and diplomatic corps

#### Stakeholder Engagement
- **Ensures** effective engagement with governmental and institutional stakeholders
- **Opens doors** to key decision-makers
- **Advocates** for member interests at the highest levels
- **Builds** strategic alliances with major organizations

### Role Within the Project Management System

#### Access and Permissions
- **View-only access** to high-level strategic dashboards
  - Annual progress against strategic goals
  - Financial summary (revenue, expenses, reserves)
  - Member growth and retention
  - Major event outcomes
- **No involvement** in day-to-day operational details

#### Approval Authority
- **Final approval** for:
  - Annual strategic plan
  - Annual budget (>€50,000 items)
  - Major partnerships and contracts
  - Final project reports at key milestones
- **Approval timeline**: Within 2 weeks of submission

#### Reporting
- **Receives**:
  - Quarterly strategic reports from CEO
  - Annual comprehensive report
  - Ad-hoc briefings on major issues
- **Participates in**:
  - Quarterly board meetings (if formal board exists)
  - Annual general assembly

### Key Relationships

- **Reports to**: Association governing body or membership (depending on legal structure)
- **Works closely with**: Vice President, CEO
- **Engages with**: Government ministers, ambassadors, major corporate leaders

### Time Commitment

- **Variable**: Depends on specific activities and external engagements
- **Estimated**: 10-20% of full-time equivalent
- **Peak periods**: Major events, annual planning, government meetings

### Key Performance Indicators

- Quality of high-level relationships maintained
- Strategic partnerships secured
- Government support and funding obtained
- Reputation and visibility of ACIK
- Member and stakeholder satisfaction with strategic direction

## 2. Executive Oversight: Vice President

### Main Functions

#### Cross-Border Coordination
- **Coordinates** interaction between the Italian and Kazakh sides of ACIK
- **Ensures** balanced representation of both countries' interests
- **Bridges** cultural and operational differences
- **Facilitates** communication across time zones and languages

#### Quality Assurance
- **Oversees** the quality of task, plan, and event implementation
- **Monitors** timely communication to ensure achievement of required KPIs
- **Reviews** deliverables for quality and impact
- **Provides** feedback to CEO and team on performance

#### Partner Selection and Management
- **Participates** in partner selection (legal, consulting, logistics, etc.)
- **Supervises** the fulfillment of KPI targets by partners
- **Reviews** partner performance
- **Approves** major partnership agreements

#### Strategic Support
- **Advises** President on strategic matters
- **Represents** President when unavailable
- **Provides** continuity in leadership
- **Mentors** CEO and senior team members

### Role Within the Project Management System

#### Access and Permissions
- **Full access** to project monitoring tools
  - Real-time project status
  - Task completion rates
  - Budget vs. actual spending
  - Event outcomes and KPIs
- **Ability to comment** on reports and plans
- **Approve** interim reports and major deliverables

#### Financial Oversight
- **Read-only access** to financial summaries
- **Reviews** quarterly financial reports
- **Approves** expenses over €5,000 (in conjunction with CEO)
- **Monitors** overall financial health

#### Reporting
- **Receives**:
  - Weekly executive summaries from CEO
  - Monthly detailed progress reports
  - Quarterly strategic reviews
  - Ad-hoc updates on issues and opportunities

### Accountability

- **Accountable to**: President
- **Responsible for**: Operational effectiveness and quality

### Key Relationships

- **Reports to**: President
- **Supervises**: CEO (functional oversight)
- **Collaborates with**: CFO, Project Manager, key partners

### Time Commitment

- **Regular engagement**: Approximately 30-50% of full-time equivalent
- **Flexible schedule**: Can vary based on activities and needs

### Key Performance Indicators

- Quality scores for events and services
- KPI achievement rate (target: 80%+)
- Partner performance ratings
- Cross-border collaboration effectiveness
- Stakeholder satisfaction with ACIK delivery

## 3. Operational Management: CEO

### Main Functions

#### Overall Operational Leadership
- **Oversees** the operational management of all ACIK projects
- **Leads** the team (Project Manager, Marketing, Operations)
- **Ensures** day-to-day activities align with strategic goals
- **Makes** operational decisions within approved budget and strategy

#### Resource Management
- **Monitors** deadlines, resource allocation, and KPI achievement
- **Allocates** human and financial resources to priorities
- **Manages** team capacity and workload
- **Ensures** efficient use of resources

#### External Relations
- **Maintains** coordination and communication with:
  - Ministries and government agencies
  - Partners (legal, logistics, media, etc.)
  - Sponsors and funding sources
  - Major members and clients
- **Represents** ACIK in operational meetings and events

#### Performance Management
- **Tracks** progress against annual plan
- **Addresses** issues and obstacles
- **Implements** corrective actions
- **Reports** to President and Vice President on progress

### Role Within the Project Management System

#### Access and Permissions
- **Full access** to:
  - All project dashboards and active tasks
  - Team communications and documents
  - Financial data (detailed)
  - Member and stakeholder databases
- **Authority to**:
  - Create, edit, and approve tasks
  - Allocate budget within approved limits
  - Hire contractors and vendors (within budget)
  - Make operational decisions

#### Reporting Responsibilities
- **Generates** reports for President and Vice President:
  - Weekly executive summaries
  - Monthly progress reports
  - Quarterly strategic reviews
  - Annual comprehensive report
- **Presents** at board meetings and to stakeholders

#### Decision-Making Authority
- **Operational decisions**: Full authority
- **Financial decisions**: Up to €5,000 independently; €5,000-20,000 with VP approval; >€20,000 with President approval
- **Partnership agreements**: Recommend to VP/President for approval
- **Hiring**: Up to mid-level positions independently

### Accountability

- **Accountable to**: President (strategic direction) and Vice President (quality and execution)
- **Responsible for**: Achievement of annual objectives, team performance, operational excellence

### Key Relationships

- **Reports to**: President and Vice President
- **Manages**: Project Manager, Marketing Manager, Operations Team
- **Collaborates with**: CFO
- **Engages with**: Government contacts, partners, major members

### Time Commitment

- **Full-time position**

### Key Performance Indicators

- Achievement of annual objectives (strategic goals)
- Budget performance (variance <10%)
- Team performance and satisfaction
- Stakeholder satisfaction scores
- Number of successful projects delivered
- Revenue growth

## 4. Financial Management: CFO/Financial Director

### Main Functions

#### Financial Planning
- **Conducts** financial planning and budgeting
- **Develops** annual budget in collaboration with CEO
- **Creates** financial projections and scenarios
- **Plans** cash flow to ensure liquidity

#### Budget Control
- **Oversees** project budget control
- **Monitors** actual vs. planned spending
- **Identifies** variances and issues
- **Recommends** corrective actions

#### Financial Reporting
- **Prepares** financial reports related to:
  - Grants and government funding
  - Sponsors and partnerships
  - Membership contributions
  - Overall organizational finances
- **Ensures** compliance with accounting standards
- **Provides** financial analysis and insights

#### Expenditure Management
- **Approves** expenditures according to approval limits
- **Monitors** payment processes
- **Ensures** proper documentation
- **Manages** vendor relationships (financial aspects)

#### Revenue Management
- **Oversees** invoicing and collections
- **Monitors** revenue streams
- **Identifies** opportunities for revenue optimization
- **Manages** relationships with banks and financial institutions

### Role Within the Project Management System

#### Access and Permissions
- **Full access** to:
  - All project financial data
  - Budget tracking systems
  - Accounting software
  - Financial dashboards
- **Authority to**:
  - Approve budgets and expenses (within approved amounts)
  - Process payments
  - Prepare financial reports
  - Audit project expenditures

#### Limited Access
- **Limited access** to non-financial project data (tasks, timelines, etc.)
- **Participates** in project planning from financial perspective

#### Reporting Responsibilities
- **Monthly financial reports** to CEO
- **Quarterly financial reviews** to CEO, VP, President
- **Annual financial statements**
- **Grant and sponsor reports** as required

### Accountability

- **Accountable to**: President and CEO
- **Responsible for**: Financial health, compliance, accurate reporting

### Key Relationships

- **Reports to**: President and CEO (depending on org structure)
- **Collaborates with**: CEO, Project Manager (on project budgets)
- **Engages with**: Banks, auditors, tax advisors, grant agencies

### Time Commitment

- **Part-time to full-time** depending on organization size and complexity
- **Estimated**: 50-100% of full-time equivalent

### Key Performance Indicators

- Budget accuracy (variance <10%)
- Timely financial reporting (100% on time)
- Clean audits (if conducted)
- Cash flow management (no liquidity crises)
- Revenue collection efficiency (receivables <30 days)
- Grant acquisition success rate

## 5. Project Coordination: Project Manager

### Main Functions

#### Project Planning
- **Plans** individual projects from concept to completion
- **Develops** project timelines, budgets, and resource plans
- **Identifies** risks and mitigation strategies
- **Creates** detailed work breakdown structures

#### Project Execution
- **Launches** and coordinates individual projects
- **Assigns** tasks to team members
- **Manages** day-to-day project activities
- **Ensures** quality and timely delivery

#### Monitoring and Control
- **Monitors** task execution, deadlines, and KPI performance
- **Tracks** project progress against plan
- **Identifies** issues and obstacles
- **Implements** corrective actions

#### Communication and Coordination
- **Facilitates** communication between internal teams
- **Coordinates** with external partners
- **Manages** stakeholder expectations
- **Reports** progress to CEO and leadership

### Role Within the Project Management System

#### Access and Permissions
- **Full access** to:
  - All project tasks and timelines
  - Project management software
  - Event planning tools
  - Communication platforms
- **Authorized to**:
  - Assign executors to tasks
  - Update task statuses
  - Create new tasks within approved projects
  - Communicate with stakeholders on project matters

#### Reporting Responsibilities
- **Weekly status reports** to CEO
- **Project completion reports** after each event/initiative
- **Monthly** progress summaries
- **Issue escalation** to CEO as needed

### Accountability

- **Accountable to**: CEO and Vice President
- **Responsible for**: On-time, on-budget, quality delivery of projects

### Key Relationships

- **Reports to**: CEO
- **Manages/Coordinates**: Marketing Team, Operations Team (for specific projects)
- **Collaborates with**: CFO (on budgets), external partners
- **Engages with**: Members, event participants, vendors

### Time Commitment

- **Full-time position**

### Key Performance Indicators

- Project on-time delivery rate (target: 90%+)
- Project budget performance (variance <10%)
- Stakeholder satisfaction with projects (target: 80%+)
- Number of successful projects delivered
- Issue resolution time
- Team satisfaction with project management

## Succession Planning

### Key Person Risk Mitigation

For each critical role, ACIK maintains:
1. **Documented responsibilities** and processes
2. **Cross-training** for key functions
3. **Identified successor** or succession process
4. **Knowledge transfer** protocols

### Succession Scenarios

- **President**: Vice President typically assumes role temporarily; formal process for permanent appointment
- **CEO**: Vice President or senior team member (PM) assumes interim role; search for permanent CEO
- **Other Roles**: Internal promotion or external hiring depending on circumstances
