# 6. Project Timeline, Human & Financial Resources

## Resource Planning and Project Cycle Management

---

## Overview

ACIK follows a **cyclical project management approach** that ensures continuous planning, execution, monitoring, and improvement. This document outlines the project cycle phases, timeline management, and resource allocation framework.

## Phase 1: Planning Phase

### Objective
Establish clear project parameters, allocate resources, and prepare for successful execution.

### Key Activities

#### 1.1 Timeline and Deadline Setting
- **Annual Planning**: Create comprehensive activity plan for the year
- **Quarterly Breakdown**: Divide annual plan into quarterly objectives
- **Monthly Scheduling**: Detail monthly activities and milestones
- **Event Calendar**: Schedule all major events in advance
- **Buffer Time**: Include contingency time for unexpected delays

#### 1.2 Budget Planning
- **Revenue Projections**: Estimate income from various sources
  - Membership fees
  - Event sponsorships
  - Consulting services
  - Government grants
  - Partnership income
- **Expense Planning**: Detail all anticipated costs
  - Personnel costs
  - Event expenses (venues, catering, materials)
  - Marketing and communication
  - Travel and logistics
  - Administrative overheads
  - Technology and tools
- **Contingency Reserve**: Maintain 10-15% reserve for unexpected expenses
- **Cash Flow Management**: Plan monthly cash flow to ensure liquidity

#### 1.3 Role Assignment and Responsibility Distribution

**Marketing Team** (Micaela and Sara):
- Content creation (articles, interviews, podcasts)
- Social media management (LinkedIn, Telegram, Instagram)
- Website updates
- Event promotion
- Brand management
- Media relations

**Operational Team** (Olzhas, Saule, Dimash):
- Logistics coordination
- Event execution
- Hospitality services
- Administrative support
- Vendor management
- On-ground operations in Kazakhstan

**Project Management**:
- Overall project coordination
- Timeline management
- Stakeholder communication
- KPI tracking
- Risk management

**CEO**:
- Strategic oversight
- Resource allocation
- Partner relations
- Government engagement
- Financial management

**President & Vice President**:
- Strategic direction
- High-level stakeholder engagement
- Final approval authority

### Deliverables
- Annual Activity Plan (see document 07)
- Detailed budget with quarterly breakdown
- Role and responsibility matrix (RACI chart)
- Project timeline with milestones
- Risk register

### Timeline
- **Annual Planning**: December (for following year)
- **Quarterly Planning**: Last month of previous quarter
- **Monthly Planning**: Last week of previous month

## Phase 2: Execution Phase

### Objective
Implement planned activities according to schedule, budget, and quality standards.

### Key Activities

#### 2.1 Task Execution
- Implement planned activities as scheduled
- Coordinate between teams for integrated delivery
- Maintain quality standards
- Manage stakeholder expectations
- Adapt to changing circumstances as needed

#### 2.2 Resource Deployment
- Allocate human resources to specific tasks
- Release budget funds according to plan
- Engage external partners and vendors
- Utilize technology and tools effectively

#### 2.3 Documentation
- Record all activities and decisions
- Maintain project files and documents
- Track expenses against budget
- Document lessons learned in real-time

### Deliverables
- Completed activities and projects
- Event reports
- Marketing materials and content
- Service delivery to members and clients
- Financial transactions recorded

### Timeline
- **Ongoing**: According to approved project schedules
- **Event-Specific**: As per event timelines
- **Service Delivery**: As per client agreements

---

## Phase 3: Monitoring & Controlling Phase

### Objective
Ensure projects stay on track and identify issues early for timely resolution.

### Key Activities

#### 3.1 Weekly Team Updates and Meetings
- **Day**: Every Friday
- **Format**: Team meeting (in-person or virtual)
- **Duration**: 60-90 minutes
- **Agenda**:
  - Task status updates from each team
  - Issues and blockers
  - Upcoming activities for next week
  - Resource needs
  - Quick decisions on operational matters

#### 3.2 Task Status Reports
- **Frequency**: Weekly (submitted before Friday meeting)
- **Content**:
  - Completed tasks
  - In-progress tasks with % completion
  - Planned tasks for next week
  - Challenges and risks
  - Support needed
- **Format**: Standardized template (see templates section)

#### 3.3 Brainstorming Sessions for Continuous Improvement
- **Frequency**: As needed (minimum monthly)
- **Trigger**: When challenges arise or new opportunities emerge
- **Participants**: Relevant team members + leadership
- **Output**: Action items and improvement initiatives

#### 3.4 Adjustments to Plans Based on Feedback and Performance
- **Performance Review**: Compare actuals vs. plans
- **Feedback Integration**: Incorporate stakeholder feedback
- **Plan Modifications**: Adjust timelines, budgets, or approaches
- **Communication**: Inform all stakeholders of changes

### Deliverables
- Weekly status reports
- Updated project schedules
- Risk mitigation actions
- Corrective action plans
- Continuous improvement initiatives

### Monitoring Tools and KPIs

#### Project Management KPIs
- **On-Time Delivery**: % of tasks completed by deadline
- **Budget Variance**: Actual spend vs. planned budget
- **Resource Utilization**: % of team capacity utilized
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction**: Survey scores (target: 80%+)

#### Operational KPIs
- **Events**: Number organized, attendance, satisfaction scores
- **Business Development**: B2B meetings, partnerships facilitated
- **Marketing**: Content produced, engagement rates, reach
- **Membership**: Number of members, retention rate

### Timeline
- **Weekly Monitoring**: Every Friday
- **Monthly Review**: First week of each month
- **Quarterly Assessment**: Within two weeks of quarter end

---

## Phase 4: Mid-Term Evaluation and Reporting

### Objective
Conduct comprehensive assessment of progress, gather feedback, and make strategic adjustments.

### Key Activities

#### 4.1 Progress Reports to CEO and President
- **Frequency**: Periodically (suggested: quarterly or semi-annually)
- **Content**:
  - Executive summary
  - Progress against strategic goals
  - Key achievements and highlights
  - Challenges and issues
  - Financial summary
  - Stakeholder feedback
  - Recommendations for adjustments

#### 4.2 Stakeholder Feedback Collection
- **Members**: Satisfaction surveys and focus groups
- **Partners**: Partnership review meetings
- **Government**: Briefing sessions and impact reports
- **Event Participants**: Post-event surveys

#### 4.3 Lessons Learned Documentation
- **What Worked Well**: Successful approaches and best practices
- **What Didn't Work**: Failures and challenges
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Understanding why things went wrong
- **Action Items**: Specific improvements to implement

#### 4.4 Strategic Adjustments
- **Goal Refinement**: Adjust goals based on performance and feedback
- **Resource Reallocation**: Shift resources to higher-priority activities
- **Process Improvements**: Implement lessons learned
- **Strategic Pivots**: Major changes if needed based on market conditions

### Deliverables
- Comprehensive progress report
- Stakeholder feedback summary
- Lessons learned document
- Revised strategic plan (if needed)
- Updated budget and resource allocation

### Timeline
- **Quarterly Evaluation**: Within 2 weeks of quarter end
- **Semi-Annual Review**: End of June and December
- **Annual Review**: January (for previous year)

## Financial Resources

### Revenue Streams

1. **Membership Fees** (Target: 40% of revenue)
   - Corporate memberships
   - Associate memberships
   - Annual renewal revenue

2. **Event Sponsorships** (Target: 30% of revenue)
   - Forum sponsorships
   - Event sponsorships
   - Partnership packages

3. **Consulting Services** (Target: 15% of revenue)
   - Legal and financial consulting fees
   - Market entry support services
   - Project management services

4. **Government Grants** (Target: 10% of revenue)
   - Italian government support
   - Kazakh government programs
   - EU funding programs

5. **Other Income** (Target: 5% of revenue)
   - Co-working space rental
   - Podcast sponsorships
   - Content monetization

### Expense Categories

1. **Personnel** (50-60% of budget)
   - Salaries and benefits
   - Professional development
   - Contractor fees

2. **Events** (20-25% of budget)
   - Venue rental
   - Catering
   - Materials and equipment
   - Speaker fees

3. **Marketing** (10-15% of budget)
   - Content production
   - Advertising
   - Website and digital tools
   - Design and branding

4. **Operations** (10-15% of budget)
   - Office rent (including co-working space)
   - Technology and software
   - Travel
   - Administrative expenses

5. **Contingency** (5-10% of budget)
   - Emergency reserve
   - Unexpected opportunities

## Resource Optimization Strategies

1. **Partnerships**: Leverage partner resources to reduce costs
2. **Sponsorships**: Secure event sponsors to offset event costs
3. **Digital Tools**: Use efficient project management and collaboration tools
4. **Shared Resources**: Collaborate with other associations to share costs
5. **Volunteer Support**: Engage interns or volunteers for specific projects
