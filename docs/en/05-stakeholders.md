## 1. Business Stakeholders

### Description
Private sector companies and entrepreneurs from Italy and Kazakhstan seeking to expand their business activities.

### Subcategories

#### 1.1 Italian Companies
- **SMEs**: Small and medium enterprises looking for new markets
- **Large Corporations**: Multinationals with expansion strategies
- **Startups**: Innovative companies seeking partnerships
- **Family Businesses**: Traditional Italian businesses exploring opportunities

#### 1.2 Kazakh Companies
- **State Enterprises**: Government-owned companies
- **Private Companies**: Kazakh businesses seeking Italian partners
- **Joint Ventures**: Existing Italian-Kazakh partnerships
- **Entrepreneurs**: Individual business owners

### Interests and Needs
- Market information and intelligence
- Business partner identification
- Networking opportunities
- Legal and regulatory guidance
- Logistics and operational support

### Engagement Methods
- B2B meetings and matchmaking
- Sector-specific events
- Trade missions
- Consulting services
- Regular communications (newsletters, updates)

### Communication Channels
- LinkedIn (professional networking)
- Email (direct communications)
- Website (information hub)
- In-person meetings at events

### Value Proposition
*"ACIK connects you with vetted partners, provides market intelligence, and supports your business expansion with practical services."*

---

## 2. Government Stakeholders

### Description
Government bodies, ministries, and agencies in both Italy and Kazakhstan that support trade and economic cooperation.

### Key Entities

#### 2.1 Italian Government
- **Ministry of Foreign Affairs**: Diplomatic relations and trade promotion
- **Ministry of Economic Development**: Industrial policy and business support
- **ICE (Italian Trade Agency)**: Official trade promotion agency
- **Regional Governments**: Support for regional businesses
- **Italian Embassy in Kazakhstan**: On-ground diplomatic presence

#### 2.2 Kazakh Government
- **Ministry of Foreign Affairs**: International relations
- **Ministry of Trade and Integration**: Trade policy and facilitation
- **KAZAKH INVEST**: Investment promotion agency
- **Regional Akimats**: Local government bodies
- **Kazakh Embassy in Italy**: Diplomatic representation

### Interests and Needs
- Facilitate bilateral trade growth
- Attract foreign investment
- Support national companies in international expansion
- Strengthen diplomatic and economic ties
- Implement trade agreements

### Engagement Methods
- Official meetings and briefings
- Policy dialogue and roundtables
- Joint event organization
- Progress reports and impact assessments
- Participation in government-led initiatives

### Communication Channels
- Official correspondence
- Institutional meetings
- Joint press releases
- Government forums and conferences

### Value Proposition
*"ACIK serves as a practical implementation partner for government trade promotion strategies, delivering measurable results in bilateral cooperation."*

## 3. Association and Chamber Stakeholders

### Description
Other business associations, chambers of commerce, and industry bodies that share similar objectives.

### Key Partners

#### 3.1 Italian Associations
- **Confindustria**: General Confederation of Italian Industry
- **Confcommercio**: Italian General Confederation of Trade, Tourism and Services
- **Regional Chambers of Commerce**: Local business support organizations
- **Sector-Specific Associations**: Industry associations (e.g., construction, energy, manufacturing)

#### 3.2 Kazakh Associations
- **National Chamber of Entrepreneurs (Atameken)**: Main business association in Kazakhstan
- **Sector Associations**: Industry-specific organizations
- **Foreign Investors Council**: Platform for foreign businesses

#### 3.3 International Organizations
- **European Business Association (in Kazakhstan)**: EU companies in Kazakhstan
- **International Chambers of Commerce**: Global business network
- **Bilateral Chambers**: Other country-specific chambers in Kazakhstan

### Interests and Needs
- Collaboration on events and initiatives
- Information sharing
- Member value enhancement
- Market access for members
- Policy advocacy

### Engagement Methods
- Partnership agreements
- Co-organized events
- Joint advocacy initiatives
- Cross-membership benefits
- Information exchange

### Communication Channels
- Inter-organization meetings
- Joint newsletters
- Co-branded events
- Formal partnership agreements

### Value Proposition
*"ACIK offers specialized expertise in Italian-Kazakh relations, complementing broader business support services and expanding value for all members."*

## 4. Member Stakeholders

### Description
Companies and organizations that have officially joined ACIK as members, paying membership fees and actively participating in association activities.

### Member Categories

#### 4.1 Corporate Members
- Full membership with voting rights
- Access to all services and events
- Priority in B2B matchmaking
- Representation in governance

#### 4.2 Associate Members
- Limited membership benefits
- Access to select events
- Information services
- Networking opportunities

#### 4.3 Honorary Members
- Special recognition for contributions
- Advisory role
- Access to events

### Interests and Needs
- Return on membership investment
- Exclusive benefits and services
- Networking with peers
- Business opportunities
- Voice in association strategy

### Engagement Methods
- Regular member communications
- Exclusive member events
- Priority service access
- Member surveys and feedback
- General Assembly participation

### Communication Channels
- Member portal (if available)
- Dedicated email communications
- Member-only events
- Direct relationship management

### Value Proposition
*"ACIK membership provides exclusive access to business opportunities, market intelligence, and a community of like-minded businesses committed to Italian-Kazakh cooperation."*

## Stakeholder Communication Plan

### Weekly
- Member updates (internal newsletter)
- Social media posts (public engagement)

### Monthly
- Business opportunity alerts
- Event announcements
- Partner coordination meetings

### Quarterly
- Government briefings
- Association partnership reviews
- Member satisfaction surveys

### Annually
- Annual Report to all stakeholders
- General Assembly for members
- Strategic review with government partners

## Stakeholder Feedback Mechanisms

1. **Surveys**: Regular satisfaction surveys for members and event participants
2. **Direct Feedback**: Open channels for comments and suggestions
3. **Focus Groups**: Periodic discussions with key stakeholder groups
4. **Performance Reviews**: Formal assessments with government and major partners
5. **Social Media Monitoring**: Tracking public sentiment and engagement
