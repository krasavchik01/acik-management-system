# 4. Project Objectives – How We Achieve Our Goals

## Operational Activities and Service Delivery

---

## Overview

This document outlines the **specific operational activities** ACIK undertakes to achieve its strategic goals. These objectives represent the day-to-day work that delivers value to stakeholders.

---

## Objective 1: Business Development

### Description

Search for and identify business partners, facilitate B2B connections, and create opportunities for commercial collaboration between Italian and Kazakh companies.

### Key Services

#### 1.1 Business Matchmaking

- **Partner Search**: Identify suitable business partners based on specific criteria
- **Company Profiling**: Research and profile potential partners
- **Introduction Services**: Facilitate initial contact between interested parties
- **Negotiation Support**: Provide assistance during early-stage negotiations

#### 1.2 B2B Meeting Organization

- **Event-Based Meetings**: Coordinate structured B2B sessions during forums and events
- **Ad-Hoc Meetings**: Arrange individual meetings based on member requests
- **Virtual Meetings**: Facilitate online introductions when physical meetings aren't possible
- **Follow-Up Support**: Track outcomes and provide post-meeting assistance

#### 1.3 Information Services About Business Opportunities

- **Market Reports**: Prepare sector-specific market analyses
- **Investment Opportunities**: Identify and present concrete investment projects
- **Tender Notifications**: Alert members to relevant public tenders
- **Regulatory Updates**: Provide information on legal and regulatory changes

#### 1.4 Scouting of Investment-Funded Projects

- **Project Database**: Maintain a database of investment opportunities
- **Due Diligence Support**: Assist with preliminary project assessment
- **Funding Navigation**: Help identify appropriate funding sources
- **Project Presentation**: Create materials to present opportunities to investors

### Deliverables

- Quarterly matchmaking reports
- Minimum 50 B2B meetings facilitated annually
- Monthly business opportunity newsletters
- Database of 100+ vetted investment projects

### Responsible Team

- **Lead**: CEO
- **Support**: Project Manager, Marketing Team

## Objective 2: Consulting and Services

### Description

Provide specialized consulting services to help companies navigate legal, financial, and operational challenges when doing business across borders.

### Key Services

#### 2.1 Legal Consulting

- **Corporate Law**: Advice on company formation and structure
- **Contract Review**: Analysis of commercial agreements
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Guidance on Italian and Kazakh regulations
- **Intellectual Property**: Support for trademark and patent matters

#### 2.2 Financial Consulting

- **Financial Modeling**: Assistance with business case development
- **Banking Connections**: Introductions to financial institutions
- **Payment Solutions**: Advice on cross-border payment mechanisms
- **Tax Guidance**: Basic tax planning information (with partner tax advisors)

#### 2.3 Management Consulting

- **Market Entry Strategy**: Support for companies entering new markets
- **Operational Setup**: Guidance on establishing local presence
- **HR and Recruitment**: Connections to recruitment services
- **Supply Chain**: Assistance with logistics and supply chain setup

#### 2.4 Support for Company Formation and Administrative Processes

- **Registration Support**: Assistance with company registration procedures
- **License Applications**: Help obtaining necessary business licenses
- **Administrative Navigation**: Guidance through bureaucratic processes
- **Document Preparation**: Support preparing required documentation

### Deliverables

- Consulting reports and recommendations
- Partner referral network (legal, financial, HR)
- Administrative process guides
- Minimum 20 consulting engagements annually

### Responsible Team

- **Lead**: CEO
- **Support**: Operational Team, External Partners

---

## Objective 3: Event Organization

### Description

Host and coordinate high-quality events that bring together stakeholders, showcase opportunities, and facilitate meaningful connections.

### Key Services

#### 3.1 Business Events and Forums

- **Annual Forum**: Flagship event with 100+ participants
- **Sector-Specific Events**: Focused gatherings for particular industries
- **Networking Receptions**: Informal events for relationship building
- **Online Webinars**: Virtual events for broader reach

#### 3.2 Roadshows

- **Italian Roadshows**: Multi-city presentations in Italy about Kazakhstan
- **Kazakh Roadshows**: Presentations in Kazakhstan about Italian opportunities
- **Sector Roadshows**: Industry-specific promotional tours
- **Investment Roadshows**: Presentations of specific investment opportunities

#### 3.3 Site Visits

- **Company Visits**: Organized tours of factories and facilities
- **Infrastructure Tours**: Visits to major infrastructure projects
- **Cultural Sites**: Combined business and cultural visits
- **Government Meetings**: Facilitated meetings with officials

#### 3.4 Trade Missions

- **Italian Trade Missions to Kazakhstan**: Organized delegations of Italian businesses
- **Kazakh Trade Missions to Italy**: Delegations of Kazakh businesses
- **Sector-Specific Missions**: Focused on particular industries
- **Government-Led Missions**: Coordination with official trade delegations

### Deliverables

- Minimum 15 events annually (mix of formats)
- Event reports with attendance and outcomes
- Post-event follow-up and matchmaking
- Annual calendar of events published in advance

### Responsible Team

- **Lead**: Project Manager
- **Support**: Marketing Team, Operational Team

## Objective 4: Logistics and Hospitality

### Description

Provide comprehensive logistical support to ensure smooth execution of events, visits, and delegations.

### Key Services

#### 4.1 Travel Arrangements

- **Flight Bookings**: Coordination of air travel
- **Visa Support**: Assistance with visa application processes
- **Travel Insurance**: Information and arrangement of insurance
- **Ground Transportation**: Airport transfers and local transport

#### 4.2 Accommodation

- **Hotel Bookings**: Securing appropriate accommodations
- **Negotiated Rates**: Leveraging partnerships for discounted rates
- **Long-Term Stays**: Arrangements for extended business trips
- **Alternative Accommodations**: Apartments or serviced residences when appropriate

#### 4.3 Full Logistics Service During Business Events

- **Venue Selection**: Identifying and booking appropriate venues
- **Catering**: Arranging meals and refreshments
- **Equipment**: Audio-visual and technical equipment provision
- **Interpretation**: Arranging translation and interpretation services
- **Materials**: Preparing and distributing event materials
- **Registration**: Managing attendee registration and check-in

### Deliverables

- Full logistics packages for all ACIK events
- Hospitality support for minimum 50 visitors annually
- Partnerships with hotels, transport providers, and venues
- Logistics handbook for common procedures

### Responsible Team

- **Lead**: Operational Team (Olzhas, Saule, Dimash)
- **Support**: Project Manager

## Objective 5: Marketing and Communication

### Description

Maintain strong visibility for ACIK and its activities through strategic content creation and distribution across multiple channels.

### Key Services

#### 5.1 Content Creation

- **Articles**: News articles, opinion pieces, and analytical content
- **Interviews**: Interviews with business leaders and experts
- **Podcasts**: Regular podcast series featuring success stories and insights
- **Videos**: Promotional and educational video content
- **Photography**: Professional documentation of events and activities
- **Branded Media**: Customized content for members and partners

#### 5.2 Digital Presence Management

- **Website**: Regular updates to www.acik.org (or relevant domain)
- **LinkedIn**: Professional networking and B2B content
- **Telegram**: Quick updates and community engagement
- **Instagram**: Visual storytelling and cultural content
- **Email Newsletters**: Regular updates to members and stakeholders

#### 5.3 News and Updates

- **Press Releases**: Official announcements to media
- **Member Updates**: Regular communications to ACIK members
- **Event Promotion**: Marketing for upcoming events
- **Success Stories**: Case studies of successful partnerships

#### 5.4 Targeted Campaigns

- **Member Recruitment**: Campaigns to attract new members
- **Event Promotion**: Specific campaigns for major events
- **Thematic Campaigns**: Focus on particular sectors or initiatives
- **Partner Campaigns**: Co-branded content with partners

### Deliverables

- Minimum 50 content pieces annually (articles, interviews, podcasts)
- Weekly social media posts across all platforms
- Monthly newsletter to members and subscribers
- Quarterly campaign reports with engagement metrics

### Responsible Team

- **Lead**: Marketing Team (Micaela, Sara)
- **Support**: All teams provide content and information
