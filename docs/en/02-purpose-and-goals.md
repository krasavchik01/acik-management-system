# 2. Purpose of ACIK

## Mission and Vision

---

## Core Purpose

**ACIK (Italian-Kazakh Trade Association)** is dedicated to fostering strong, sustainable relations between Kazakhstan and Italy across all areas of mutual interest.

### Our Mission

To serve as a **bridge for promoting collaboration** in:

- **Trade** - Facilitating commercial exchanges and business partnerships
- **Investment** - Supporting cross-border investment opportunities
- **Innovation** - Promoting technology transfer and innovative solutions
- **Technology** - Enabling access to advanced technologies and expertise
- **Culture** - Strengthening cultural understanding and appreciation
- **Tourism** - Promoting travel and tourism between both nations

## Vision Statement

> *To be the premier platform connecting Italian and Kazakh businesses, governments, and societies, creating lasting value through sustainable bilateral cooperation.*

## Core Values

### 1. **Partnership**
We believe in the power of collaboration. Every relationship we build is based on mutual respect, trust, and shared benefit.

### 2. **Excellence**
We strive for the highest quality in everything we do, from organizing events to providing consulting services.

### 3. **Integrity**
We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical standards in all our interactions with members, partners, and stakeholders.

### 4. **Innovation**
We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to continuously improve and stay relevant in a changing world.

### 5. **Cultural Sensitivity**
We respect and celebrate the unique cultural heritage of both Italy and Kazakhstan, using cultural understanding as a foundation for stronger business relationships.

### 6. **Sustainability**
We focus on long-term impact, ensuring that our activities contribute to sustainable development in both countries.

## Why ACIK Exists

### The Challenge

- Italian businesses lack comprehensive knowledge about Kazakhstan's market opportunities
- Kazakh companies need access to Italian technology, expertise, and markets
- Cultural and administrative barriers hinder potential partnerships
- Limited platforms exist for systematic bilateral cooperation

### The Solution: ACIK

ACIK addresses these challenges by:

1. **Information Provision**: Offering reliable, up-to-date market intelligence
2. **Network Building**: Creating connections between relevant parties
3. **Facilitation Services**: Smoothing administrative and logistical processes
4. **Advocacy**: Representing member interests to governments and institutions
5. **Knowledge Sharing**: Disseminating best practices and success stories

## Long-Term Goals

Based on the **Aims of the Association**, our long-term goals focus on:

### 1. Deepening Economic Ties
- Increase bilateral trade volume year-over-year
- Facilitate investment flows in both directions
- Support the establishment of joint ventures

### 2. Building Institutional Capacity
- Develop a robust network of members and partners
- Create sustainable revenue streams
- Build organizational knowledge and expertise

### 3. Enhancing Visibility
- Position ACIK as the go-to organization for Italian-Kazakh relations
- Increase brand recognition in both countries
- Attract high-level institutional support

### 4. Creating Social Impact
- Promote cultural exchange programs
- Support educational initiatives
- Foster people-to-people connections

### 5. Driving Innovation
- Support technology transfer projects
- Promote green energy and sustainable business practices
- Facilitate knowledge exchange in key industries

## Target Outcomes

By 2026, ACIK aims to achieve:

- **100+ active members** (companies and organizations)
- **10+ successful business partnerships** facilitated annually
- **20+ events** (forums, roadshows, site visits) per year
- **Strong institutional relationships** with government bodies in both countries
- **Sustainable financial model** with diversified revenue sources
- **High stakeholder satisfaction** (80%+ positive feedback)

## Alignment with National Strategies

### Kazakhstan's Strategic Interests
- Attracting foreign investment
- Technology and knowledge transfer
- Economic diversification
- International integration

### Italy's Strategic Interests
- Expanding into Central Asian markets
- Securing energy and resource partnerships
- Promoting Italian excellence (technology, design, manufacturing)
- Strengthening geopolitical ties in the region

ACIK operates at the **intersection of these interests**, creating win-win opportunities.
