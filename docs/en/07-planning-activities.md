# 7. Planning Activities (Proposal)

## ACIK Activity Plan 2025-2026

---

## Overview

This document outlines the **proposed activity plan for ACIK** for the period 2025-2026. These activities translate strategic goals into concrete, executable initiatives.

## Annual Activity Calendar: Key Initiatives

### 1. Launching a New Flight Atyrau-Milan-Atyrau Event

**Date**: November 18, 2025
**Location**: Atyrau, Kazakhstan

#### Objective
Celebrate and promote the new direct flight connection between Atyrau and Milan, facilitating easier travel for business and tourism.

#### Key Activities
- Official launch ceremony with government and airline representatives
- Business networking reception
- Media coverage and press conference
- B2B meetings with local businesses
- Presentation of business opportunities in Atyrau region

#### Target Audience
- Italian businesses interested in Kazakhstan's energy sector
- Kazakh companies in Atyrau region
- Travel and tourism industry representatives
- Government officials from both countries
- Media representatives

#### Success Metrics
- 100+ attendees at launch event
- 20+ B2B meetings organized
- National media coverage in both countries
- Increased flight bookings by Italian businesses

#### Responsible Team
- **Lead**: Project Manager
- **Support**: Operational Team (Olzhas, Saule, Dimash), Marketing Team
- **Partners**: Airline (Neos), Local Akimat, Italian Embassy

#### Budget Estimate
- Venue and catering: $5,000-7,000
- Marketing and promotion: $2,000-3,000
- Logistics and hospitality: $3,000-4,000
- **Total**: $10,000-14,000

### 5. Coworking Space (in Almaty)

**Launch Date**: Q2 2025
**Location**: Almaty, Kazakhstan

#### Objective
Establish a physical presence in Almaty that serves as:
- ACIK office and meeting space
- Venue for events and networking
- Coworking space for Italian companies entering Kazakhstan market
- Revenue-generating asset

#### Services Offered
- Hot desks and dedicated desks for rent
- Private meeting rooms
- Event space for up to 50 people
- Business support services (printing, reception)
- Coffee/tea and basic amenities

#### Revenue Model
- **Monthly coworking memberships**: $200-500/month per desk
- **Meeting room rentals**: $50-100/hour
- **Event space rental**: $500-1,000/day
- **ACIK member discounts**: 20% off standard rates

#### Target Users
- Italian companies setting up in Kazakhstan
- ACIK members visiting Almaty
- Local startups and entrepreneurs
- Event organizers

#### Success Metrics
- 80%+ occupancy rate within 6 months
- Break-even within 12 months
- 10+ events hosted per quarter
- Positive user satisfaction (80%+)

#### Responsible Team
- **Lead**: CEO (strategic oversight)
- **Operations**: Operational Team (Olzhas, Saule, Dimash)
- **Marketing**: Marketing Team

#### Budget Estimate
- Initial setup (furniture, equipment): $15,000-25,000
- Monthly rent: $2,000-3,000
- Monthly operations: $1,000-1,500
- First year costs: $50,000-70,000
- Expected revenue (Year 1): $30,000-40,000
- **Net Cost Year 1**: $10,000-40,000

**Frequency**: 3-4 visits per year
**Partners**: Tomiris Tour + Neos

#### Objective
Facilitate direct exposure to business opportunities through organized site visits and trade missions.

#### Types of Visits

**Italian Companies to Kazakhstan**
- Site visits to factories and projects

**Kazakh Companies to Italy**

- **Day 2**: Business presentations, meetings in Almaty

- **Day 4**: Travel to Astana, government meetings
- **Day 5**: Business forum and B2B meetings
- **Day 6**: Optional: Cultural program
- **Day 7**: Departure

#### Success Metrics
- 3-4 missions per year
- 10-15 participants per mission
- 80%+ satisfaction rate
- 50%+ of participants engage in follow-up business activities

#### Responsible Team
- **Lead**: Project Manager
- **Logistics**: Operational Team, Tomiris Tour
- **Support**: Marketing Team for promotion

#### Budget Estimate (per delegation)
- Flights (group rates): $800-1,200 per person
- Accommodation (6 nights): $600-900 per person
- Local transport and logistics: $200-300 per person
- Meals and events: $300-400 per person
- **Total per person**: $1,900-2,800
- **For delegation of 12**: $23,000-34,000

#### Revenue Model
- Participants pay: $2,500-3,500 per person
- Sponsors cover: $5,000-10,000
