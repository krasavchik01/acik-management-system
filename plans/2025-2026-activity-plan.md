## Executive Summary

This document outlines ACIK's comprehensive activity plan for 2025-2026, translating strategic goals into concrete, executable initiatives that will strengthen Italian-Kazakh business relations and deliver value to all stakeholders.

**Total Budget**: €259,000-381,000
**Expected Revenue**: €256,000-385,000

## Risk Management

### Key Risks and Mitigation

**1. Economic Downturn**
- Risk: Reduced member revenue
- Mitigation: Diversified revenue streams, reserves, value proposition focus

**2. Lower Than Expected Event Attendance**
- Risk: Events undersubscribed
- Mitigation: Early marketing, VIP invitations, compelling programs

**3. Coworking Space Underutilization**
- Risk: Low occupancy
- Mitigation: Aggressive marketing, flexible pricing, member benefits

**4. Budget Overruns**
- Risk: Expenses exceed plan
- Mitigation: Monthly monitoring, contingency reserves, approval controls

**5. Key Person Dependency**
- Risk: Loss of critical staff
- Mitigation: Documentation, cross-training, succession planning
