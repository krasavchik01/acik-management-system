## Italian-Kazakh Trade Association
### Project Management Proposal

---

## Overview

This repository contains the comprehensive Project Management System for ACIK (Italian-Kazakh Trade Association). The system is designed to unify internal teams and external partners around a structured process that ensures effectiveness, clarity, and sustainability in achieving the association's strategic goals.

## Purpose

ACIK is dedicated to fostering strong, sustainable relations between Kazakhstan and Italy across all areas of mutual interest. The association serves as a bridge for promoting collaboration in:

- Trade and Investment
- Innovation and Technology
- Culture and Tourism
- Economic Cooperation

## Key Features

- **Bilingual System**: Full documentation in English and Russian
- **Role-Based Access**: Clear responsibilities and accountability
- **Structured Workflow**: Planning → Execution → Monitoring → Evaluation
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Multi-level involvement framework
- **Continuous Improvement**: Built-in evaluation and learning mechanisms

## Reporting Structure

- **Weekly Updates**: Every Friday - Team status reports
- **Monthly Reports**: End of month - Comprehensive progress review
- **Mid-Term Evaluation**: Periodic reports to CEO and President
- **Annual Review**: Year-end assessment and planning

## Contact & Support

For questions regarding this project management system, please contact:

- **CEO**: Operational management and coordination
- **Project Manager**: Day-to-day project execution
- **Vice President**: Strategic oversight and quality assurance
